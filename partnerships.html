Indigenous Partnerships

Indigenous Partnerships and Community Commitment

As a majority First Nations-owned company, Loyalty 6 Energy Services is deeply rooted in the values of collaboration, respect, and community. We are committed to fostering meaningful partnerships with Indigenous communities and actively contributing to long-term, mutually beneficial relationships.

Our team includes valued First Nations employees, and we continue to promote Indigenous participation in our workforce through inclusive hiring practices and skill development opportunities. Through our structured On-the-Job (OTJ) training program, new workers receive hands-on experience under the mentorship of seasoned professionals—ensuring safety, confidence, and growth in the field.

Beyond employment, Loyalty 6 invests in Indigenous communities by supporting local events, contributing to infrastructure projects, and encouraging broader economic engagement. Our goal is to not only deliver quality energy services, but to also play a positive role in the communities we serve.
