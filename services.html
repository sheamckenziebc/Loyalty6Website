Our Services

Delivering Expertise and Reliability Across the Energy Sector

Pipefitting and General Crew Truck Services

Loyalty 6 Energy Services specializes in providing high-quality pipefitting and crew truck services, the core of our operations. Our team brings extensive industry expertise, built on years of hands-on experience across the oil and gas sectors of Northern British Columbia and Alberta.

We offer a full roster of skilled pipefitters and field crews capable of supporting a wide range of project requirements. Loyalty 6 is committed to delivering responsive, reliable service — our crews are available 24/7 and prepared to mobilize wherever and whenever our clients need us.

Our areas of specialization include the construction, maintenance, repair, and decommissioning of oil and gas wellsites and facilities. If your project involves oil and gas infrastructure, Loyalty 6 Energy Services has the capability and experience to get the job done efficiently and safely.

We are committed to delivering every project with exceptional quality, professionalism, and reliable workmanship. Our priority is to complete all work safely, on schedule, and within budget.

Construction

At Loyalty 6 Energy Services, we support the planning and execution of projects of all sizes — from small-scale initiatives to major facility new construction. Whether it's a complete build or something in between, our team is equipped to ensure seamless project delivery.

Maintenance

For ongoing preventative maintenance, we can follow your existing program or assist in developing a comprehensive new plan tailored to your operation's needs. We recognize the impact that delays can have in the oil and gas industry. That's why, during maintenance operations, we focus on minimizing downtime. On every project, Loyalty 6 Energy Services works diligently to keep your operations running efficiently and without disruption.

Turnarounds

We support the planning and execution of major facility turnarounds. During turnaround operations, we focus on minimizing downtime and expediting return to full production. On every project, Loyalty 6 Energy Services works diligently to get your facilities back online quickly and safely.

Mechanical Work & Pipefitting

As specialists in pipefitting and mechanical services, our skilled crews handle the repair and maintenance of oil and gas wellsites and facilities. We ensure all mechanical work meets rigorous safety and quality standards.

General Labour

Loyalty 6 Energy Services provides adaptable and skilled general labour support across British Columbia and Alberta. Our crews are equipped to assist with essential site tasks such as preparation, cleanup, material handling, and providing reliable backup for specialized trades, ensuring your project runs smoothly.

We supply safety-trained and dependable labourers ready to integrate seamlessly into your project workflow. Understanding the dynamic needs of oil and gas and industrial projects, our teams are committed to efficiency, safety, and contributing effectively to maintain a productive worksite.

Whether you require short-term assistance or ongoing site support, Loyalty 6 delivers versatile labour solutions tailored to your operational requirements.

Hot Shot Services

Loyalty 6 Energy Services proudly offers reliable hotshot, delivery, and pilot truck services throughout British Columbia and Alberta. Whether it's urgent equipment, critical parts, or scheduled deliveries, our team ensures that your materials arrive safely and on time—every time.

We maintain a diverse fleet of well-maintained trailers and transport units to accommodate a wide variety of load requirements. From light-duty hauls to oversized and specialized freight, we are equipped to handle the unique demands of the oil and gas sector and other industrial operations.

Our experienced drivers and dispatch team understand the importance of timely logistics in high-stakes environments. We are committed to safety, efficiency, and clear communication from dispatch to delivery.
